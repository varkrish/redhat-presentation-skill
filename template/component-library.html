Component: Challenge List

Key Challenges & Blockers

Pain points identified across participants — each item is distinct and actionable

  • Skills Gap (60%): Teams lack playbook authoring skills. Classroom training alone is insufficient — they need embedded, hands-on co-creation.
  • Cultural Resistance: Siloed teams and approval-heavy processes block adoption even when leadership and budget are aligned.
  • Pilot-to-Production Gap: Subscriptions purchased but stuck between PoC and production — no roadmap, no champion, no governance to scale.
  • Integration Complexity: Existing ITSM tools and change management processes create friction when automating across teams.
